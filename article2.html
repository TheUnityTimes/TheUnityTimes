Description of play and characters

- Alex Kotov - 3 Minute Read.

Unity (1918)

"Unity (1918)" is a play that is about the year 1918. 1918 was a time when war was happening worldwide, and the Spanish Flu was spreading rapidly. The Spanish Flu targets people who were young and elderly, and the characters in this book are at a prime age to get it. The pandemic and war brings fear into the town of Unity and eventually creates conflict between the citizens.

Town of Unity

The town of Unity is located in Saskatchewan, Canada and has a population of around 600 in 1918. Unity has a big fear of the Spanish Flu throughout the play. To protect from the flu, the town goes through quarantine and many other measures such as burning mail from foreign countries.

Beatrice Wilde

Beatrice Wilde is a farm girl who is 21 years old, a prime age for Influenza in 1918. Beatrice has a sister Sissy and best friend Mary who both have fear of Influenza. Beatrice keeps a diary on her which she talks about all throughout the play. Through the war, Beatrice also was busy knitting socks for Glen, a beloved soldier fighting overseas.

Sissy Wilde

Sissy Wilde is Beatrice's younger sister. Sissy thinks her sister is a massive pain, always wanting to make her decisions and won't let Sissy live her own life in ways. Even though she dislikes Beatrice quite a bit, she knows she loves her.

Mary

Mary is Beatrice's Best friend and grows up in an upper-class family. Mary and Beatrice in the story both await their beloved soldiers from war oversea.

Sunna Thorson

Sunna is a 15 year old girl who struggles to fit in with the rest of the townspeople. Sunna becomes a mortician after her fathers death.

Hart

Hart is a wounded soldier who wears a cloth around his eyes because he is blind. In the play he comes back to his hometown wounded finding out his father had died.
